Machine Learning Approach For Predicting End Price Of Online Auction

ABOUT

An online auction has become one of the fastest growing modes of online commerce transaction, sellers and buyers have started preferring to go online for purchasing and selling products respectively.

As eBay has been the leading online auction marketplace for nearly two decades, the proposed system collected huge auction data from eBay and machine learning algorithms are used to predict end price of auction items.

Machine learning is widespread in computer science and other fields. Machine learning algorithms are feasible and cost effective as they figure out how to perform important task by generalizing from examples.

As eBay is the leading auction website and having free access services, data of auctions held on eBay for one month is collected. The related information about particular item is collected from official website of eBay by setting item name as a keyword.
